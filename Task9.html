About the Cat

Meet Luna, a charming and curious cat who loves exploring her surroundings. Luna has a sleek black coat with bright green eyes that sparkle with mischief. She enjoys climbing on furniture and has a favorite spot by the window where she watches the world outside.

Luna is known for her playful personality and affectionate behavior. She often purrs loudly when she is happy, and her playful antics bring joy to everyone around her. Whether she is chasing a toy or curling up for a nap, Luna's presence adds warmth and happiness to the home.
